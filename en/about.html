Our Story

About Us

Özerler Market

Manavgat's Established
Neighbourhood Market

Özerler Market is a family-run market and off-licence located in the heart of Manavgat, in the Çağlayan neighbourhood. Since we opened our doors, our single aim has been to deliver the products our customers need — at their freshest, as quickly as possible.

With the warmth and friendliness that neighbourhood culture brings, we know our regulars by name and their preferences by heart. Unlike the cold atmosphere of large supermarket chains, there is always a familiar face to greet you here.

We are open every single day, from 07:00 in the morning until 02:00 at night. Whether you are a local resident, a tourist enjoying a summer holiday in Manavgat, or someone with a late-night need — we are always here for you.

English is spoken at our store, and we are happy to assist visitors from all over the world.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Freshness

We replenish our deli, produce and grocery lines regularly and never sell stale products. Fresh, quality goods are non-negotiable for us.

Customer Focus

Every customer matters. WhatsApp orders are handled personally; special requests receive a response as quickly as possible.

Always Available

Our 07:00–02:00 hours were chosen deliberately — to cover everyone from the early-morning crowd to guests returning late in the evening.

Trust

We have served the same neighbourhood with the same honesty for years. We honour our quoted delivery times and are transparent about pricing.

Tourist-Friendly

Thousands of tourists visit Manavgat every summer. We communicate in English and are happy to assist international visitors.

Community Spirit

We are more than a shop — we are part of the neighbourhood. Our door is always open to neighbours, regulars and everyone who loves Manavgat.

Come Visit or Order

Wherever you are in Manavgat, we are just one WhatsApp message away.
